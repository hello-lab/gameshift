## üöÄ Getting Started

- Play challenging word and strategy games

5. **Log in** with your new credentials
6. **Welcome to Gameshift!** üéâ

## üë• Teams

### What are Teams?

Teams allow you to:
- **Form groups** with other players
- **Compete collectively** on team leaderboards
- **Share team scores** (aggregate of member contributions)
- **Play as a leader** (only team leaders can play Wordle)
- **Collaborate** with friends

### Creating a Team

1. **Navigate to the PARTY (Teams) section**
2. **Click "Create Team"**
3. **Fill in team details:**
   - **Team Name**: Unique and memorable (3-30 characters)
   - **Description**: Optional (describe your team's purpose)
4. **Click Create**
5. You become the **Team Leader** automatically

### Joining a Team

1. **Navigate to the PARTY section**
2. **Look for "Join Team" or team search**
3. **Find a team** you want to join
4. **Click Join Request** or **Join**
5. **Wait for leader approval** (if required)
6. Once accepted, you're part of the team!

### Team Leadership

**Only team leaders can:**
- Play Wordle and earn points for the team
- Accept/reject join requests
- Kick members from the team
- Disband the team
- Change team settings

**Leaders earn points for:**
- Their **personal account only** (not the team)
- The **team's aggregate score** is calculated from all members' total scores

### Managing Your Team

**As Team Leader:**

1. **Accept Join Requests**
   - Team ‚Üí Pending Requests
   - Review applicants
   - Accept or decline

2. **Remove Members**
   - Team ‚Üí Members
   - Find member
   - Click "Kick"
   - Confirm

3. **Disband Team**
   - Team ‚Üí Settings
   - Click "Disband Team"
   - Confirm (members will lose team affiliation)

4. **View Team Stats**
   - Team ‚Üí Overview
   - See total team score
   - View member contributions

### Leaving a Team

1. **Go to your Team page**
2. **Click "Leave Team"**
3. **Confirm your decision**
4. You're no longer part of the team

## üéÆ Games

### Available Games

#### 1. **Wordle** üî§

**What it is:** A word-guessing game with progressive difficulty and glitch mechanics

**Who can play:** Team leaders only (leaders earn points for their personal account)

**How to play:**
- Guess the 5-letter word in limited attempts
- Color feedback guides your guesses:
  - **Green** = Correct position
  - **Yellow** = Wrong position
  - **Gray** = Not in word
- Beware of glitches that deceive you!

**Scoring:**
- 1 guess = 10 points
- 2 guesses = 8 points
- 3 guesses = 6 points
- 4 guesses = 4 points
- 5+ guesses = 2 points
- Failed word = 0 points

**Features:**
- 20 words per game session
- Increased difficulty as you progress
- Progressive glitch system makes it harder
- Can't replay solved words
- Only team leaders can play

**For detailed gameplay instructions, see [WORDLE_MANUAL.md](WORDLE_MANUAL.md)**

---

#### 2. **Battleship** ‚öì

**What it is:** A strategic naval combat game

**Who can play:** All players

**How to play:**
- Place your ships on a grid
- Take turns guessing opponent's ship locations
- First to sink all opponent ships wins!

**Game Flow:**
1. **Setup Phase**: Place your ships strategically
2. **Battle Phase**: Take turns calling coordinates
3. **Victory**: Sink all enemy ships to win

**Scoring:** Points awarded based on:
- Speed of victory
- Number of guesses used
- Win/loss record

**Strategy Tips:**
- Spread ships out
- Leave gaps between ships
- Watch opponent patterns
- Adapt based on feedback

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem:** "Invalid credentials" error
- **Solution**: Double-check your email/username and password
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Password is case-sensitive

**Problem:** "Account not found"
- **Solution**: Verify you signed up successfully
- Check your email for confirmation
- Try recovering password

**Problem:** "Session expired"
- **Solution**: Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cookies
- Try a different browser

### Game Issues

**Problem:** Game not loading
- **Solution**: Check internet connection
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Problem:** Keyboard not working in game
- **Solution**: Click on game board first to focus
- Try physical keyboard instead of on-screen
- On mobile, scroll down to see keyboard

**Problem:** Score not saving
- **Solution**: Check internet connection
- Wait for success message
- Refresh profile to confirm
- Contact support if persistent

### Team Issues

**Problem:** Can't create team
- **Solution**: Verify you're logged in
- Check unique team name requirement
- Ensure valid team name format
- Check for duplicate team name

**Problem:** Can't join team
- **Solution**: Team may be full or closed
- Request may need approval (wait for leader)
- You may already be in a team
- Leave current team first

**Problem:** Can't leave team
- **Solution**: Ensure you're not team leader
- Leaders can't leave (must disband team)
- Check network connection
- Refresh and try again

### Profile Issues

**Problem:** Stats not updating
- **Solution**: Refresh your profile page
- Check internet connection
- Wait a few moments for sync
- Log out and log back in

**Problem:** Leaderboard position wrong
- **Solution**: Leaderboard updates periodically
- Refresh page to see latest rankings
- New games may take time to process

### General Issues

**Problem:** Page loading slowly
- **Solution**: Check internet speed
- Close other browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Problem:** Game crashes
- **Solution**: Close and reopen game
- Clear browser cache
- Restart browser completely
- Try on different device

### Getting Help

If issues persist:

1. **Clear browser cache** (Ctrl+Shift+R)
2. **Log out completely**
3. **Close browser**
4. **Reopen and log in**
5. **Try again**

If still having issues:
- Contact administrator through support email
- Provide error details and screenshots
- Describe steps to reproduce issue
- Include your browser and device info
